## Resizable Areas

### Left Panel (File Explorer)
- **Location:** Between sidebar and editor
- **Default:** 256px
- **Range:** 200px - 500px

### Right Panel (Tools)
- **Location:** Right side of IDE
- **Default:** 320px
- **Range:** 250px - 600px

### Bottom Panel (Terminal)
- **Location:** Bottom of editor
- **Default:** 300px
- **Range:** 150px - 600px
